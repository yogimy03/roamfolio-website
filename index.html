How it works

Three steps to your travel diary

1

Create a Trip

Name your adventure — "Japan 2025" or "Pacific Coast Road Trip." Choose a colour and emoji to identify it on the map.

2

Log Each Journey

Add every leg: flight, train, road, hike, cruise. Search for origins and destinations. The app draws the route for you.

3

See It on the Map

All journeys appear on one beautiful map with their real visual styles — dashed arcs for flights, winding lines for roads.

Features

Everything your travel story needs

More than a map. A complete diary for every journey you've taken.

🗺️

8 Transport Modes

Flight, Road, Train, Bus, Rideshare, Cruise, Hike, Walk, Cycle — each drawn on the map with its own visual style.

📍

Stops & Waypoints

Mark every city, landmark, or café along the route. Add notes, dates, a star rating, and a mood tag for each stop.

📸

Smart Photo Linking

Link photos directly from your camera roll — no duplicates, no extra storage. Your memories stay where they are.

🏔️

Hike & GPX Import

Import GPX files from any GPS device or app. See your trail on the map with a beautiful elevation profile chart.

📊

Travel Statistics

Total distance covered, countries visited, trips logged, and a breakdown of how you've gotten around the world.

🔒

Private by Design

All data lives on your device. No accounts, no cloud upload, no ads, no tracking. Just your journeys, yours alone.

Transport Modes

Every way you travel, visualised

Each transport mode gets its own distinct line style on the map — so a glance tells you exactly how you got there.

✈️
Flight
Geodesic arc across the globe. Dashed white line — the classic flight path.
🚗
Road
Road-snapped route via Apple Maps. Solid, thick — every curve of the drive.
🚂
Train
Solid line with perpendicular tick marks — the universal railway symbol.
🚢
Cruise
Long dashes on a gentle wave path — feels exactly like the open sea.
🥾
Hike
Earthy thick dash from your GPX track. Trail pins mark summits and viewpoints.
🚶
Walk
Fine dotted line — light as a stroll, perfect for city exploring on foot.
🚴
Cycle
Medium dash — energetic, purposeful. Every kilometre of pedalling, mapped.
🚌
Bus & Rideshare
Long dashes for bus, short dashes for rideshare. Road-snapped, always accurate.
