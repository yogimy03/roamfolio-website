How it works

Three steps to your
travel diary

01

Create a Trip

Name your adventure. Choose a colour and emoji. Each trip becomes its own vivid chapter on your map.

02

Log Each Journey

Add every leg: flight, train, road, hike, cruise. Search for origins and destinations. The app draws the route for you.

03

See It on the Map

All journeys appear on one beautiful map, each with its own visual style. Dashed arcs for flights, winding lines for roads.

Features

Everything your
travel story needs

🗺️

8 Transport Modes

Flight, Road, Train, Bus, Rideshare, Cruise, Hike, Walk, Cycle. Each drawn on the map with its own distinct visual style.

📍

Stops & Waypoints

Mark every city, landmark, or café. Add notes, dates, a star rating, and a mood tag for each stop.

📸

Smart Photo Linking

Link photos from your camera roll with no duplicates and no extra storage. Your memories stay exactly where they are.

🏔️

Hike & GPX Import

Import GPX files from any GPS device. See your trail with a beautiful elevation profile chart.

📊

Travel Statistics

Total distance, countries visited, trips logged, and a full breakdown of how you've travelled the world.

🔒

Private by Design

All data lives on your device. No accounts, no cloud upload, no ads, no tracking. Just your journeys, yours alone.
